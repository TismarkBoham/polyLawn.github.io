How it works

1
Geocode
Photon/OSM converts your address to precise lat/lon coordinates.
2
Site data
Live soil properties (SoilGrids, 250 m) and 10-year climate normals (ERA5) fetched in parallel for that exact point.
3
Suitability index
Each of 17 species is scored across 6 weighted variables. Top 5 form your personalised mix.
